WEBABOUT

Nike, (stylized as NIKE) is an American athletic footwear and apparel corporation headquartered. The company was founded on January 25, 1964, as "Blue Ribbon Sports", by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight, and officially became Nike, Inc. on May 30, 1971. The company takes its name from Nike, the Greek goddess of victory. Nike markets Foamposite, Nike Skateboarding, and subsidiaries including Bauer Hockey from 1995 to 2008, and previously owned Cole Haan, Umbro, and Hurley International. As of 2020, it employed 76,700 people worldwide. In 2020, the brand alone was valued in excess of $32 billion, making it the most valuable brand among sports businesses.
